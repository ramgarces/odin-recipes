French Toast

Description

Looking to make some fluffy, delicous French toast? Well look no further. This recipe is different because it uses flour to make French toast like no other!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices of bread
