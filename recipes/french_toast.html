French Toast

Description

Looking to make some fluffy, delicous French toast? Well look no further. This recipe is different because it uses flour to make French toast like no other!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices of bread

Steps

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  4. Working in batches, cook bread on the preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on each side. Server hot.
