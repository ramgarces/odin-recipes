Bacon and Eggs

Description

Need a simple breakfast to satisfy you in the morning? Well look no further than this recipe for that quick fix!

With only a handful of ingredients you too could make a versatile, delicious breakfast that will fill your belly and leave you ready for the day to come!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a small/medium pan to medium heat.
  2. While the pan is heating up slice your bacon into pieces.
  3. Once the pan is hot enough put the pieces of bacon into the pan
  4. Cook the bacon as well as desired (I like my bacon crunchy :D)
  5. Once the bacon is cooked to your liking, crack the eggs into the pan with the bacon still in there.
  6. Scramble and mix the eggs with the bacon and cook your eggs to your liking.
  7. Right before the eggs finish cooking, add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Enjoy!
